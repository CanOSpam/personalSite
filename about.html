"Tell me about yourself."

 

That's a tough question, huh?

 

Well, I'm passionate about making stuff, that's probably what matters most to me. It doesn't really matter what, as long as I can lose myself in it and end up with something cool at the end of the day.

 

I'm always looking to learn some new skills or pick up some fresh knowledge. What it really boils down to is the fact that the more you know, the more you can learn. I learned to paint miniatures so I could play games with my friends. I learned how to airbrush so I could paint more miniatures, faster. I learned basic electronics so I could make an airbrush-fume extractor out of spare parts. Each new skill I learn builds on and is informed by past learned skills.

But, on the other hand, making for the sake of making isn't all I want to do. Sure, it's fun to have a spare room full of crafted bits and bobs, but what really matters? Well, I think that that's helping people. Bringing joy and passion into people's lives, helping those who need it, that's what life is about. If we all reach out and give each other a helping hand, that's true humanity.

 

At the end of the day, if we're fortunate, life is a pretty long journey. All I really want to get out of it is a chance to learn, make some cool stuff, and make people happy along the way. If you want to get in touch, feel free to send me a message on my contact page. I'd love to hear from you.

 

P.S. I'm looking for a job. If you're looking for a Software Engineer that likes to make stuff and learn new skills, contact me :)
